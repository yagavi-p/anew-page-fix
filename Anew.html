ABOUT US

ANEW was founded in the year 1997 in order to work with young girls and women to significantly change their lives.
We realised early on that women need not just training in employable skills but also the avenues to seek employment, and thus was born ANEWâ€™s two-pronged focus on training and placements.
At ANEW, we believe that educating a woman truly goes a long way in empowering a family, and each of our girls is a standing testament to this.
The dream for ANEW was born in the enthusiasm of our founders Mr. MK Kumar and Mrs. Lakshmi Kumar, assisted every step of the way by Vatsala Ramakrishnan and Dr. Annalakshmi.

ANEW is a reputed and award-winning NGO with its credibility consistently assured by Credibility Alliance, Give India Foundation and Guidestar India.
